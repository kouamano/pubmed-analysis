Figure\ref{fig:gr13} shows the number of unique occurrences of highly cited topics. The total number of MeSH descriptors in2023 is about30,000, but the number of occurrences of highly cited topics in each cumulative cohort never exceeds roughly3,000, indicating a concentration of such topics. To examine the turnover of highly cited topics, the proportion of newly appearing highly cited topics relative to the entire set of highly cited topics is shown in Figure\ref{fig:gr14}. No consistent temporal trend is observed; turnover is seen in the range of a few\% to severaltens of\%. Note that the high values for the cumulative cohort of 1946-1955 arise because MeSH descriptors were scarcely assigned in the preceding cumulative cohorts.
