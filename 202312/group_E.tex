Later, in order to compare highly cited papers with other papers, we form a total of four groups that include the highly cited papers. The highly cited papers constitute Group1 (G1); similarly, the set of papers whose citation counts fall in the top 25-26 percentile of the overall citation distribution is designated G2, those in the 50-51 percentile interval as G3, and those in the 75-76 percentile interval as G4.
